What this does

Enter country, crop, year, rainfall, pesticide use, and average temperature. The app builds the engineered feature rain / (temp + 0.1), one-hot encodes Area and Item (drop-first, like pandas get_dummies), scales with your saved StandardScaler, and runs the tuned DecisionTree from Training_Code1.ipynb.

  • Outputs predicted yield in hectograms per hectare (dataset target).
  • Uses crop_yield_prediction_model.pkl, crop_yield_scaler.pkl, model_features.pkl.
